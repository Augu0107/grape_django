Rules & Guidelines

Welcome to Grape!

Grape is a fan-made Miiverse-style community. To keep this a fun and safe place for everyone, please follow these rules.

1. Be Respectful

Treat all users with respect. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, and personal attacks are not allowed. This includes comments based on race, gender, religion, nationality, or sexual orientation.

2. Keep Content Appropriate

Do not post sexually explicit, violent, or otherwise inappropriate content. This is a platform for all ages. Content that would not be allowed in a Nintendo game community is not allowed here.

3. No Spam or Advertising

Do not post repetitive messages, spam, or unsolicited advertising. Commercial advertisements are not permitted.

4. Protect Privacy

Do not share personal information of yourself or others, including real names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses.

5. No Spoilers Without Tags

When posting spoilers about games, movies, or other media, always use the spoiler tag. Be considerate of those who haven't experienced the content yet.

6. Stay On Topic

Post in the appropriate community for your content. Posts in the wrong community may be removed.

7. Account Rules

  • One account per person. Multiple accounts are not allowed.
  • Do not share your account credentials with others.
  • Account usernames must not impersonate real people or brands.

8. Consequences

Violations of these rules may result in post removal, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans at the discretion of administrators.

9. Have Fun!

Grape exists to be a fun, nostalgic community. Enjoy connecting with others who share your interests!

These rules may be updated at any time. Continued use of Grape constitutes acceptance of the current rules.
